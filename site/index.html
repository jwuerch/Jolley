Meet the Doctors

Craig R. Jolley, DMD

Doctor Craig Jolley would like to welcome you to a fantastic dental experience! By combining advanced dental technology with a gentle, friendly style, Dr. Jolley and his team will make you and your family more comfortable than you ever thought possible. Providing some of the latest advances in dental technology, including laser dentistry, digital x-rays, and dental implants, each year Dr. Jolley attends extensive postdoctoral studies in family dentistry and advanced restorative techniques.

Best of all, Dr. Jolley combines this "big city" clinical dentistry with "small town" care and communication that will make you feel right at home. You will get down-to-earth, clear explanations and the latest in advanced dentistry. It's the best of both worlds, and it really is something to smile about!

Dr. Jolley earned his doctorate from the prestigious Oregon Health and Science University in Portland. He and his wife, have been married since 1993 and have 4 children. They enjoy an active lifestyle with their family and love living in the Pacific Northwest.

David Do, DDS

We are excited to welcome Dr. David Do as the newest member of the team. We are so lucky to have found him. His philosophy and approach to patient care is compatible with Dr. Jolley's. David's love of dental care fuels his desire to discuss all available options, see the patient through the entire journey and provide the highest quality treatment by "doing the right thing at the right time". His devotion to high quality care and personable approach made him a perfect addition to our office.

In a state of the art environment Dr. Do is at home. His desire to continue with the latest advancements in dental care and technology is what first attracted David to our practice. David was raised in the Bay area of California. After graduating from the University of the Pacific School of Dentistry in San Francisco he came home to be close to family again. He keeps knowledge and techniques up to date through participating in advancement courses, as well as multiple study groups with his peers.

When not in the office Dr. Do appreciates his time spent with family and friends. He is fascinated with old cinema, music, lifestyle, culture and experiencing the Pacific Northwest's beauty.
